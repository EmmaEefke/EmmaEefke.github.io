Welcome To Studio Da Braccio!
private study

Focal Points:

Violin Studies

"Da Braccio" is Italian for "of the arm;" the studio teaches exclusively the instruments of the arm, the violin and the viola.  

Private Teaching

Learning to play an instrument well is a highly individual skill.  Studio Da Braccio believes that private lessons are the optimal method for music study.  

Music Theory

Any study of music must include music theory and a plethora of other skills like sight reading, sight singing, ear training, and music history and literacy.  At Studio Da Braccio, these skills are organically incorporated into private training.
